Tech seminar on BIOCHIP

By: Vishnu Sunil

Biochips have emerged as a groundbreaking technology with the potential to revolutionize various fields, particularly in healthcare and biotechnology. This seminar dives into the world of biochips, offering a comprehensive exploration of their applications, design principles, and implications for the future.

Biochips, also known as microarrays or DNA chips, are miniature devices that integrate multiple biochemical reactions onto a single substrate, enabling high-throughput analysis of biological molecules. In this seminar, we will delve into the fundamental concepts underlying biochips, shedding light on how they function and their significance in diverse scientific disciplines.

Tech seminar on PILL CAMERA

By:Vishnu Sunil

Unveiling the Future of Medical Imaging: Exploring Pill Camera Technology Welcome to a captivating seminar that delves into the revolutionary realm of pill camera technology, a game-changing innovation in the field of medical imaging. This seminar offers an in-depth exploration of the design, functionality, applications, and impact of pill cameras in modern healthcare. The pill camera, also known as a wireless capsule endoscope, has transformed the way medical professionals visualize and diagnose conditions within the gastrointestinal (GI) tract. This seminar aims to shed light on the science behind this remarkable technology, its potential applications beyond GI imaging, and the implications it holds for the future of medical diagnostics.
